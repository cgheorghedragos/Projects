E-YOU

Map Blog Campaigns General Ranking About us Log In Sign Up

E-YOU is an interactive platform which allows the user to signal the environmental problems they notice in the areas where they are at the moment, such as excessive pollution, wildfires or chemical hazards, signaling the competent authorities where to act in order to improve the quality of life in the communities where they live. Consequently, we can all contribute to the battle against climatic problems which we are currently facing. Together, we can make a difference!
