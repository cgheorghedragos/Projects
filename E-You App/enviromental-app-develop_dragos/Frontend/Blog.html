How to become a prosumer?

7 September 2022

The full definition of the prosumer can be found in GEO 143 of 28.12.2021. In short, to be a prosumer you must:

  1. Own a photovoltaic power plant in your location;
  2. Be able to deliver the surplus of electricity produced from renewable sources to the National Energy System ;
  3. Not having as your main commercial activity the sale of renewable energy (production for own consumption, not for sale).

What are the requirements that a prosumer should fulfil in order to benefit from the compensation or regularisation mechanisms provided by law?

  1. The main activity of the prosumer is not electricity generation (generation for own consumption, not for trading).
  2. The electrical scheme of the photovoltaic power plant is designed in such a way that both the energy supply of the prosumer from the electricity grid and the delivery of the energy produced by the photovoltaic power plant to the grid are made through the same connection.
  3. The electricity metering system is realized either with smart meters or with meters that allow at least remote reading, which can be integrated in smart electricity metering systems.

Cum să ai o locuința eficientă energetic?

7 September 2022

An energy-efficient home is one that uses as little energy as possible for lighting, heating and cooling. In recent years, building green homes has become an important concern for builders and developers from both environmental and economic perspectives.
All over the world, people have become increasingly interested in low energy housing, which is approaching zero energy consumption. There is already a European directive on this subject, which will be enforced on the last day of 2020.This directive concerns the construction of passive houses, which provide thermal comfort with low energy consumption.
Passive houses have the highest energy efficiency standard in the world and aim to keep total heat, hot water and electricity consumption below 42 kWh/m² per year. Of course, a passive house with this level of consumption per year is a tough target to reach, but that doesn't mean we can't make our homes more efficient gradually each year until we reach the target.
Energy efficiency is not just about insulating your home, but also about having a heating system that relies on renewable energy, and a ventilation system that extracts and filters stale air without losing heat in the process.

How do we recycle correctly?

7 September 2022

Recycling materials is extremely important for the protection of the environment. That is why the correct collection of products for recycling should be a basic rule for every company and for each of us. Unfortunately, many people do not know which materials can be recycled or do not follow the guidelines for the correct collection of packaging that will end up at specialised centres.
In this article we present the information you need to know about proper recycling, both on the collection of packaging and on which materials can be recycled and which will not be accepted.
So, depending on the material, some items can be recycled more than once - some recycle very easily and some not at all. On the other hand, some types of materials are not generally recycled because this activity cannot be economically supported. Cleaning packaging before disposal (at least by rinsing with water) is a must!
