CRR
4/11/2022

CRR is a campaign taking place in schools, which is centered around educating children about the topic of reusing and recycling and raising awareness about the importance of taking care of their surroundings by learning how to recycle and minimize waste. If you want your school to be apart of the campaign or if you are a teacher and want to teach your children about collecting, recycling and reusing, contact us♡
