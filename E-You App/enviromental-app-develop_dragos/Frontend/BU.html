Bucegi
4/11/2022

This fall, on the 4th of November, at 10AM, an ecologizing action will be carried out in Bucegi, in the National Reservation of Bucegi, in order to restore the beauty of this wonderful area. The littering of the tourists deeply affects its overall image and health, so we invite you to join our recycling and decluttering initiative! Big or small, we will work together to make sure that nature is protected and the gathered litter arrives in the correct recycling bins.
